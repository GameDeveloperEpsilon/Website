Texas A&M University - Kingsville Water Level Research

Sponsored by NSF - CREST

This research focuses on the water level in The Winter Garden Agricultural Region. Three sensors have been placed around this region which allows for the study of the water level with respect to the numerous factors which can influence it.

This is a map of the Winter Garden Agricultural region in Texas. The red circles are the locations of the sensors. To select a sensor, please click on it.
